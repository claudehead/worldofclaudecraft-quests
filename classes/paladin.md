## Specializations

- **Holy** — _Healer_. Signature ability: **Flash of Light** (learned at level 12).
- **Protection** — _Tank_. Signature ability: **Righteous Fury** (learned at level 16).
- **Retribution** — _DPS_. Signature ability: **Judgement** (learned at level 4).
