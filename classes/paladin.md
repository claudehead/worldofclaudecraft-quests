- **Sacrament** — _Healer_. Signature ability: **Holy Shock** (learned at level 10).
- **Vigil** — _Tank_. Signature ability: **Hallowed Wall** (learned at level 10).
- **Requital** — _DPS_. Signature ability: **Crusader Strike** (learned at level 10).
