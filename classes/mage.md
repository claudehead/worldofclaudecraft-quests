## Specializations

- **Chronomancy** — _Healer_. Signature ability: **Temporal Mend** (learned at level 5).
- **Pyromancy** — _DPS_. Signature ability: **Pyrelance** (learned at level 5).
- **Cryomancy** — _DPS_. Signature ability: **Ice Lance** (learned at level 5).
