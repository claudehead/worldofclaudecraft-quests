## Story

> The mire prowlers have learned what a supply mule sounds like, and now they hunt the causeway itself. Last week they dragged a courier into the reeds not fifty paces from this gate. Thin them out, <your name> — twelve dead prowlers ought to teach the rest fear.

## How to complete

- **Kill 12× [Mire Prowler](bestiary.md#mob-mire_prowler)** (level 7–8)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:-40, z:230 (7 mobs, radius 22)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:35, z:225 (6 mobs, radius 20)
  - _Tracker: Mire Prowler slain_

Then return to **Warden Fenwick**, Warden of Fenbridge _(at ~x:3, z:304)_ to turn in.

## Rewards

- **XP:** 800
- **Money:** 300 copper

## On completion

> Twelve, and not a bite on you? The causeway breathes easier tonight.
