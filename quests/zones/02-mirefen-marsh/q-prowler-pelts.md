|---|---|

| **Quest giver** | **Provisioner Hale**, Provisioner _(at ~x:-4, z:308)_ |

## Story

> Every plank of that causeway rests on pilings wrapped in oiled prowler hide — the only thing the rot will not chew through. My stock is gone and the south spans are already sinking. Bring me 8 unspoiled pelts, <your name>, before we are all wading to Eastbrook.

## How to complete

- **Collect 8× Mire Prowler Pelt**
  - Drops from **Mire Prowler** (60% chance) — Found in the open world at ~x:-40, z:230 (7 mobs, radius 22); Found in the open world at ~x:35, z:225 (6 mobs, radius 20)
  - _Tracker: Mire Prowler Pelt_

Then return to **Provisioner Hale**, Provisioner _(at ~x:-4, z:308)_ to turn in.

## Rewards

- **XP:** 850
- **Money:** 350 copper

## On completion

> Good thick pelts, these. The causeway will outlast the both of us now.
