> There's one troll the others won't dig beside — Grubjaw, the Glutton. He ate my last two pack-mules, harness and all, and my insurance man drowned years ago. He prowls the far eastern mounds, <your name>. Bring me his tusk and I will outfit you proper.

- **Collect 1× Grubjaw's Tusk**
  - Drops from **Grubjaw the Glutton** (100% chance) — Found in the open world at ~x:-120, z:480 (1 mob, radius 8)

Then return to **Provisioner Hale**, Provisioner _(at ~x:-4, z:308)_ to turn in.
