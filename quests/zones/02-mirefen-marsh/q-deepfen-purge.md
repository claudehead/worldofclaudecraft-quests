## Story

> Aldric says those idols are cult-make — which means the murlocs are hauling the marsh's old evil up one armful at a time. I will not have it washing onto my causeway. Go back to the shallows and break the dredging for good: 14 more snappers.

## How to complete

- **Kill 14× Deepfen Snapper** (level 8–9)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:-82, z:273 (8 mobs, radius 15)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:-120, z:350 (6 mobs, radius 13)
  - _Tracker: Deepfen Snapper slain_

Then return to **Warden Fenwick**, Warden of Fenbridge _(at ~x:3, z:304)_ to turn in.
