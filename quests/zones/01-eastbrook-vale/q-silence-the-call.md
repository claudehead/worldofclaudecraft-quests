## Story

> Every name in that ledger is a soul Morthen means to drag from the earth, and the chapel yard already crawls with those he has called. Return 12 Restless Bones to their graves, <your name>, before the Gravecaller's whisper swells into a chorus.

## How to complete

- **Kill 12× Restless Bones** (level 5–7)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:80, z:78 (8 mobs, radius 18)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:88, z:90 (2 mobs, radius 6)
  - _Tracker: Restless Bones silenced_

Then return to **Brother Aldric**, Priest of the Vale _(at ~x:-14, z:-10)_ to turn in.

## Rewards

- **XP:** 750
