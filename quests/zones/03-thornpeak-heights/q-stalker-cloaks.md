## Story

> Eight pelts lined the officers' cloaks, and now every soldier on the wall wants the same, <your name>. They are right to want it: winter takes fingers first and apologies never. Ten more pelts from the ridges south of the gate, and the whole watch sleeps warm.

## How to complete

- **Collect 10× Ridge Stalker Pelt**
  - Drops from [**Ridge Stalker**](bestiary.md#mob-ridge_stalker) (60% chance) — Found in the open world at ~x:-50, z:590 (7 mobs, radius 22); Found in the open world at ~x:45, z:600 (6 mobs, radius 20)
  - Drops from [**Ridge Stalker**](bestiary.md#mob-ridge_stalker) (60% chance) — Found in the open world at ~x:-50, z:590 (7 mobs, radius 22); Found in the open world at ~x:45, z:600 (6 mobs, radius 20)
  - _Tracker: Ridge Stalker Pelt_

Then return to **Quartermaster Bree**, Highwatch Quartermaster _(at ~x:-5, z:668)_ to turn in.
