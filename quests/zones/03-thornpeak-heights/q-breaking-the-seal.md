## Story

> The seal on the Sanctum was wrought with mountain-fire, and only mountain-fire will let us pass without tearing it wide open. The stormcrag elementals carry embers of that first forging in their cores. Bring me five Blessed Embers, <your name> — for if the cult opens that gate first, they will not be careful, and the Wyrm will not wake gently.

## How to complete

- **Collect 5× Blessed Embers**
  - Drops from [**Stormcrag Elemental**](bestiary.md#mob-stormcrag_elemental) (55% chance) — Found in the open world at ~x:110, z:760 (8 mobs, radius 20); Found in the open world at ~x:135, z:795 (6 mobs, radius 16)
  - _Tracker: Blessed Embers_

Then return to **Brother Aldric**, Priest of the Vale _(at ~x:-10, z:656)_ to turn in.

## Rewards

- **XP:** 4200
- **Money:** 2200 copper
