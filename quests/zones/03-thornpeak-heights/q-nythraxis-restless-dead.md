## Story

> Something has changed in Thornpeak Heights, <your name>. The dead no longer wander aimlessly. They gather and march through the northern bonefields beyond Highwatch, where the old battlefield meets the cliff road. Go there, investigate the unrest among the Boneclad Revenants, and bring back any proof of what is driving them.
