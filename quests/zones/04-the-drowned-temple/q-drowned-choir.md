## Story

> The waders do not act alone. Among them walk the Drowned Votaries — the cult that sank with the temple, still in their rotted vestments, still singing the prayer from the shore-rocks. Silence eight of them, and bring me six of the offerings they carry. I would know what they mean to give their goddess.

## How to complete

- **Kill 8× [Drowned Votary](bestiary.md#mob-drowned_votary)** (level 15–16)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:-90, z:802 (7 mobs, radius 16)
  - Found in the open world at ~x:-64, z:814 (5 mobs, radius 12)
  - _Tracker: Drowned Votary silenced_
- **Collect 6× Drowned Offering**
  - Drops from [**Drowned Votary**](bestiary.md#mob-drowned_votary) (60% chance) — Found in the open world at ~x:-90, z:802 (7 mobs, radius 16); Found in the open world at ~x:-64, z:814 (5 mobs, radius 12)
  - _Tracker: Drowned Offering_

Then return to **Ondrel Vane**, Tidewatcher _(at ~x:-66, z:786)_ to turn in.

## Rewards

- **XP:** 3800
- **Money:** 1800 copper
- **Item reward (by class):**
  - Tidewatcher's Wraps — _warrior, mage, rogue_

## On completion

> Pearls, knuckle-bones, a child's carved fish... grave-gifts, $N. They are not raising the dead. They are dressing them, the way you dress a body for burial. The temple is a tomb that refuses to close.
