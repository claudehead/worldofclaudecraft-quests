# Death, Ghosts & Graveyards

Since **v0.20**, dying in World of Claudecraft works the WoW way: you leave a **corpse** where you fell and become a **ghost**. You choose how to come back.

## What happens when you die

1. Your body drops as a **corpse** at the spot you died.
2. You appear as a **ghost** at the **nearest graveyard**, where a **Spirit Healer** (an angel) hovers.
3. As a ghost you **cannot interact with the world** — no looting, fighting, or using objects. Logging out preserves the death state; you're still a ghost when you return.

## Two ways to resurrect

- **Corpse run (free).** Run your ghost back to your corpse and reclaim your body. No penalty — this is the standard, cost-free option.
- **Spirit Healer (instant, but costs).** Accept the Spirit Healer's resurrection to revive **on the spot** at the graveyard. The price is **Resurrection Sickness** — a temporary debuff that weakens you for a while. Use it when your corpse is somewhere dangerous or far away.

> Tip: the corpse run is almost always worth it while leveling — Resurrection Sickness makes the next few minutes much harder. Save the Spirit Healer for when the corpse is unreachable (mid-boss, over a cliff, deep in a pack).

## Graveyards

A Spirit Healer waits at every graveyard below. When you die, you're sent to the nearest one. They're marked on the [world map](#/map) and rendered in the [3D zone maps](#/zone3d).
