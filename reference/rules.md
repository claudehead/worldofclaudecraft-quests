# Rules & Conduct

Account and fair-play rules for World of Claudecraft. **Breaking these can get your account banned**, so read before you push the limits.

## Multi-boxing & accounts
- ✅ **You may play 2 characters at once** — but only from the **same account**.
- ✅ **Only 1 account at a time.** Running more than one account simultaneously is not allowed.

## No automation — every input must be manual
- ⛔ **Automation is forbidden.** Bots, scripts, macros, key-broadcasting, input replay, or any tool that plays for you are **not allowed**.
- ✅ **Every input must be performed manually**, by a human, in real time. This applies to *both* characters when dual-boxing — you control them by hand, not via automation.

## Penalty
- **Infractions may result in a ban.** When in doubt, don't risk your account.

---

> **Note for tinkerers:** the [open-source project](https://github.com/levy-street/world-of-claudecraft) ships a pure, deterministic offline simulation and an RL/agent interface (`src/sim/obs.ts`). Experiment with agents there — in **offline / practice mode**, against your own local build — never against the **live** service or a real account. The live server is authoritative and runs anti-bot protection; automating it violates the rules above.
