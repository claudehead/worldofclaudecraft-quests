# Fishing

Buy a **Simple Fishing Pole** from the Provisioner in the first village (about 20 copper), equip it, then use it at any lake or river. Each cast rolls the local water's catch table below. Raw fish are **cooked into food** that restores health over time — the bigger the fish, the more it heals.

> The **Glimmerfin Koi** is the prize catch — a rare that can surface in any water. Keep casting.

## Eastbrook Vale

| Catch | Chance | Restores |
|---|---:|---|
| Raw Mirror Trout | 45% | 61 HP |
| Raw River Perch | 30% | 45 HP |
| Tangled Weed | 12% | — |
| _nothing (the one that got away)_ | 10% | — |
| Glimmerfin Koi ⭐ | 3% | 117 HP |
