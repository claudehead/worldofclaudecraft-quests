# Getting started

New to World of Claudecraft? This walks you from zero to questing, grouping, and trading — plus fixes for lag.

## 1. Move around

Click into the game window first so it captures your keyboard, then:

- **Walk** with **`W` `A` `S` `D`** (or the **arrow keys**).
- **Jump** with **`Space`**.
- **Look around** by holding the **right mouse button** and dragging; scroll to zoom.

## 2. Start a quest (click an NPC)

- Walk up to an **NPC with a quest marker** above their head and **click them** to talk.
- Read the offer and **accept** it. Your active quests live in the **Quest Log** (`L`).
- Do the objective (kill / collect / interact), then **click the turn-in NPC** to hand it in.
- Not sure where to go? Use this guide's **[Quests](#/quests)** and **[Leveling route](#/route)** — every quest has a "where to go" map.

## 3. Group & trade with real players

- **Click another player** to target them and open their menu.
- Choose **Invite to Party** to group up (they get a prompt to accept), or **Trade** to swap items and coin.
- In a trade window, drag items in and set coin, then both players confirm.
- Party members nearby **share quest credit and XP** (with a group XP bonus).

## 4. Chat & party chat

Press **`Enter`** to open chat, type, and press **`Enter`** to send. Prefix the line to pick a channel:

| Type this | Goes to |
|---|---|
| `/s message` (or `/say`) | **Say** — players nearby |
| `/y message` (or `/yell`) | **Yell** — the whole area |
| `/w name message` (or `/tell`) | **Whisper** one player privately |
| `/p message` (or `/party`) | **Party** chat |
| `/g message` | **Guild** chat |
| `/general message` | **General** — the zone-wide channel |
| `/world message` | **World** channel |
| `/lfg message` | **Looking-for-group** channel |

> Manage your guild and friends from the **Social** panel (friends / guild / ignore tabs).

## 5. Running slow? Fix lag

Most stutter is the browser, not the game. In order:

1. **Play in an Incognito / Private window.** This disables browser extensions, which are the most common cause of lag and input glitches.
2. **Turn graphics down to Medium.** The game defaults to **Ultra**. Open **Settings** and set the graphics preset to **Medium** (or **Low**). Instant trick: add **`?gfx=medium`** to the page URL and reload (also `?gfx=low` / `?gfx=high`).
3. **Use a Chromium browser** (Chrome/Edge) and **close other heavy tabs**.
4. On a laptop, plug in to mains and set the OS to **High performance** so the GPU isn't throttled.
