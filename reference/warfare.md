# Honor & Warfare — the PvP gear track ⚔️

v0.25 adds a PvP progression track. You earn **Honor** from ranked arena and the Fiesta, and spend it with the **Honor Quartermaster** on **Warfare** gear — armor and weapons carrying a PvP-only offense/defense rating that only matters against other players.

> ⚔️ **The basics:** win PvP → earn **Honor** → buy **Warfare** gear from **FURY** at the arena. Honor is the currency; **Warfare** is the offense/defense rating stat on the gear, which boosts your damage and cuts incoming damage *in player-vs-player only* (it does nothing in PvE).

---

## Earning Honor

| Source | Honor |
|---|---|
| Ranked arena win — **1v1** | 25 |
| Ranked arena win — **2v2** | 50 |
| Fiesta kill | 20 |
| Fiesta completion | 20 |
| Fiesta win bonus | +40 |

**Anti-farm rules** stop you grinding the same target:

- **Same-team arena wins** only pay the **first** win against a given opponent each day.
- **Daily taper:** full rate for your first 10 arena wins a day, then half rate through win 14, then quarter rate from win 15. Counters reset at the UTC-day rollover.
- **Repeat Fiesta pairings** diminish — full, half, quarter, then nothing on the fourth repeat.

There's **no rank ladder or PvP title track** here — Honor is purely a gear currency. (For arena Elo and ranked play, see the [Coliseum guide](#/doc/reference%2Fprotect-yumi.md).)

---

## What "Warfare" means

Every piece of PvP gear carries two ratings — **PvP Offense** and **PvP Defense**. Ten rating equals 1%, each capped at **±20%**. In a fight against another player your damage is multiplied by `(1 + offense) × (1 − defense)` — so a fully-geared player hits ~20% harder and takes ~20% less from other players. **None of it applies to PvE**: against monsters the ratings are inert.

So think of it as two things: **Honor** is what you grind, and **Warfare** is the PvP-only power you buy with it.

---

## The vendor — FURY

All Warfare gear is sold by **FURY, the Honor Quartermaster**, at the arena. *"The sands remember every victory. Spend your honor well."*

There are **four armor sets** (one per armor type / stat profile), plus necklaces, rings and main-hand weapons — every piece a level-20 epic:

| Set | Armor | For |
|---|---|---|
| **Furyforged Battlegear** | mail | Strength / Stamina |
| **Stormbound Vestments** | mail | Int / Stamina / Spirit |
| **Ashstalker Kit** | leather | Agility / Stamina |
| **Cinderweave Regalia** | cloth | Int / Stamina / Spirit |

**Roughly what things cost (in Honor):**

- **Chest** 700 · **Legs** 600 · **Head / Shoulders** 500 / 400 · **Waist / Hands / Feet** 250–300
- **Necklaces** 225 · **Rings** 150
- **Main-hand weapons** 800 — the single biggest sink (a 2H greatblade, a dagger, or a caster staff, each class-locked)

Prioritise the **chest and legs** for the most rating per Honor, and save toward a weapon last.
