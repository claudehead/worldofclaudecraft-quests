# Heroic Nythraxis — the 10-player raid ⚔️

v0.25 gives the endgame raid a **Heroic tier**. **Nythraxis, Scourge of Thornpeak** — the 10-player fight beyond the sealed royal door in Thornpeak Heights — now has a harder difficulty that layers the **Deathless Court** onto the normal script and drops raid-tier heroic gear plus three heroic-only weapons.

> ⚔️ **The basics:** convert to a **raid group**, complete the attunement quest, and zone in. Heroic adds a summoned trio of spirits (the **Deathless Court**) and a punishing raid-heal channel you *must* crowd-control. Beat it once per daily reset for the best non-crafted loot in the game.

---

## Getting in

- **Raid group required** — you must convert your party to a raid before the royal door will admit you.
- **Attunement** — you need the quest **"Nythraxis, Bound Guardian"** completed. It's the tail of the Thornpeak crypt chain (Restless Dead → Graves → Sealed Crypt → Bound Guardian → Scourge's End). Without it: *"The royal door is sealed to you."*
- **No backing out** — once Nythraxis is engaged the door seals and reopens only on his death or a full wipe.

---

## The fight (heroic layer)

The normal encounter is a two-phase fight — a **Gravebreaker** frontal cone and raised adds in phase one, a transition at 70% HP into phase two with **Soul Rend**, **Deathless Rage** and arena wardstones, and a **Final Stand** enrage at 5%. Heroic keeps all of that and turns the dials up:

| Mechanic | Normal | Heroic |
|---|---|---|
| **Soul Rend** marks | 3 players | **6 players** (stack to split) |
| **Deathless Rage** (if not interrupted) | 82% max HP | **115% max HP** — a wipe |
| **Dread Curse** on the tank | — | **+10% damage taken per stack**, every 15s, up to 10 stacks |
| **The Deathless Court** | — | a summoned trio of spirits |

**Interrupt Deathless Rage** by soaking the three arena **wardstones** — each needs a *different* player to finish a 5-second channel; complete all three at once and the boss is stunned for 5 seconds.

---

## The Deathless Court & Malric's Mending

Each Deathless Rage cycle, Nythraxis raises his court — **Spirit of Aldren** (warrior), **Spirit of Malric** (priest) and **Spirit of Voss** (rogue). Aldren is immune to crowd control; **Malric and Voss can be CC'd**.

**Spirit of Malric is the whole fight.** He channels **Malric's Mending** — an escalating heal on the boss that ramps every few seconds toward a cap so high that *no raid can out-damage it*. The source is blunt about it: even a max-geared raid cannot burn through the capped channel. So:

> 🛑 **Malric must be crowd-controlled.** A stun, incapacitate or silence resets his ramp. He's deliberately squishy — you *can* burn him — but CC is the intended answer, and letting the channel reach cap means the boss heals faster than ten players can damage him.

Keep Malric locked down, split Soul Rend, soak the wardstones to stop Deathless Rage, and hold the tank alive through Dread Curse stacks.

## Heroic rewards

Every heroic boss drop **auto-upgrades** to its raid-tier heroic variant (epics at item level 33, legendaries at 37) — you don't hunt separate pieces, the loot swaps on a heroic kill. On top of that, exactly **one of three heroic-only weapons** drops per kill:

| Weapon | Type | Stats | For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Deathless Greatblade** | 2H (45–68 dmg, 3.4 spd) | +14 Str, +9 Sta | Warrior / Paladin / Shaman |
| **Scepter of the Deathless Court** | 1H caster (29–51, 2.4) | +13 Int, +10 Spi | Mage / Priest / Warlock / Druid |
| **Stormcaller's Focus** | 1H (31–52, 2.5) | +14 Int, +9 Spi | Paladin / Shaman |

---

## Lockout

Nythraxis is on a **daily raid reset** (realm-local 3 AM), and the lockout is **difficulty-scoped** — a heroic kill locks only the heroic version, so you can still run it on normal the same day, and vice versa. The lock catches everyone involved: released ghosts, door-campers and party-leavers are all locked, though a defeated raider can re-enter their own live instance for a corpse run.

**The associated deeds:** *Heroic: Scourge No More* (25 Renown), *None More Deathless* — clear it with zero raider deaths (50, title **"the Deathless"**), and it counts toward **Deepward** (the Heroic-clear badge border).
