# Raids & Dungeons

Instanced group content — private copies of a zone you and your party clear for elite gear and quest objectives. This is the overview + strategy guide; each instance also has its own detail page with a route map, full pull list, and loot (linked below).

## Dungeons vs Raids

| Format | Party size | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| 🧍 **Solo** | 1 | Story lead-ins you can clear alone. |
| ⚔️ **Dungeon** | up to 5 | Standard group instances — a few elite pulls and a boss or two. |
| 🐉 **Raid** | up to 10 | Large-scale encounters with mechanics that punish mistakes (see boss mechanics below). |

- **Instances are private.** Entering takes your group into a separate copy, so you never compete with other players for pulls or loot.
- **Mobs leash.** Pulled enemies give up and reset if you drag them more than **70 yards** from their spawn — pull deliberately; don't over-run.
- **Party XP & loot** are shared with nearby members — stick together so everyone gets credit.
- **Bring a balanced group** for dungeons/raids: someone to hold aggro, someone to heal, and damage. Solo lead-ins are tuned for one player.

## How to run an instance

1. **Get the right level & group.** Match the level range above; fill the party for dungeons/raids.
2. **Pull deliberately.** Clear trash toward the boss one pack at a time. If a fight goes bad, run — mobs leash and reset at 70 yards.
3. **Mind the tags.** **Boss** > Elite > Rare > Normal in danger and reward. Bosses hit hardest and drop the best gear.
4. **Loot the boss.** Instance bosses are the main source of best-in-slot gear — see the [Best-in-Slot](../gear/bis.json) builds and each instance page for its drop table.
5. **Watch boss cast bars.** Raid bosses have telegraphed mechanics (below). Reacting to the cast — not just DPS — is what clears them.

## Boss mechanics

### 🐉 Nythraxis — Nythraxis Raid Arena (10-player raid)
- **The pillars are the fight.** They take **time to charge**, and there is a **limited window** to use them.
- **Watch the death-attack cast** under Nythraxis's HP bar — when he starts charging it, drop everything.
- **Race to a charged pillar within the window.** If you're **too slow to the pillars, it's a wipe (GG)** — the death attack one-shots the raid.
- Priority: pillar timing **over** damage. Assign players to pillars ahead of each charge.
