# PvP — the Ashen Coliseum

Step into the Coliseum to fight other players in ranked brackets. Win and your rating climbs; lose and it dips (with a floor so a bad run can't tank you). Three formats:

| Format | Players | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| **1v1** | 2 | A straight duel — your kit vs theirs. |
| **2v2** | 4 | Two-player teams; coordination and peeling matter. |
| **Fiesta** | free-for-all | A chaotic brawl where **power-ups** spawn on the field — grab them to swing the fight. |

## Fiesta power-ups

In Fiesta, power-ups periodically spawn (with a short telegraph before they're grabbable). Run over one to claim its buff for a few seconds — they're the heart of the mode.

| Power-up | Lasts | Effect |
|---|---:|---|
| **Speed Demon** | 12s | +70% movement speed, shrinks you (harder to hit) |
| **Colossus** | 14s | grows you (bigger, slower), slow 0.82 |
| **Moon Boots** | 14s | huge jump height, +25% movement speed |
| **Berserker** | 10s | ap 280, +20% movement speed |

_Power-up effects are read from the game data; grab timing and spawn cadence are tuned by the match._
