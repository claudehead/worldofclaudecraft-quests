Paladin
Resource Mana
Roles Tank, Healer, DPS
Specs Holy (Healer) · Protection (Tank) · Retribution (DPS)

Specializations

  • Holy — Healer. Signature ability: Flash of Light (learned at level 12).
  • Protection — Tank. Signature ability: Righteous Fury (learned at level 16).
  • Retribution — DPS. Signature ability: Judgement (learned at level 4).
