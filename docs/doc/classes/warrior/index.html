Warrior
Resource Rage
Roles Tank, DPS
Specs Arms (DPS) · Fury (DPS) · Protection (Tank)

Specializations

  • Arms — DPS. Signature ability: Mortal Strike (learned at level 10).
  • Fury — DPS. Signature ability: Bloodthirst (learned at level 10).
  • Protection — Tank. Signature ability: Shield Slam (learned at level 10).
